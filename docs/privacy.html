This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Gamles (the “App”) handles information when you use it.

1) Who we are

Developer / Publisher: Catadoo (individual developer; not a registered company)

Individual: Pavle Prodanović

Contact: catadook@gmail.com

2) Scope

This policy applies to the Daily Gamles Android app and related services we operate (for example, an API used to receive aggregate metrics).

3) What information the App collects

3.1 Data you provide directly

None. The App does not require registration and does not ask you to submit personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

3.2 Data stored on your device

The App stores app data locally on your device (for example, your streaks and favorites), so the App can function.

3.3 Aggregate metrics sent to our server (global statistics)

To compute global app statistics, the App transmits limited event/usage metrics to our server, such as:

  • number of times a game was played
  • which games were added to or removed from favorites

We design these metrics to be non-personal and not linked to a user account.

Important note about technical metadata: when any device connects to a server, the server typically receives certain technical data (for example IP address and basic request metadata) as part of normal internet communications. We may process and retain such server logs for security, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.

3.4 Optional ads (Google AdMob)

The App includes an optional support button that shows a rewarded ad via Google AdMob only when you choose to watch one.

We request non-personalized ads. Even when requesting non-personalized ads, ad providers may process certain device and network data to deliver, measure, and protect ads (for example, fraud prevention).

For more information on how Google processes data in advertising, please review Google’s privacy information and AdMob documentation.

4) How we use information

We use the information described above to:

  • provide core app features (local streaks/favorites)
  • compute and display global aggregated statistics based on gameplay events
  • maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot (including server logs)
  • provide optional rewarded ads when you press the support button (AdMob)

5) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share or disclose data as follows:

  • Google AdMob (Google): when you choose to watch an ad, data is processed by the Google Mobile Ads SDK for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • Service providers / hosting: our infrastructure providers may process data on our behalf to host and operate the service (for example, receiving requests and logs).
  • Legal / safety: if required to comply with law or protect rights, safety, and security.

6) External links

When you click an app/game inside Daily Gamles, it opens that game’s website. Those websites are not operated by us, and their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. You should review them before using those sites.

7) Your choices and controls

  • Optional ads: you can use the App without watching ads.
  • Non-personalized ads: we request non-personalized ads; however, ad providers may still process certain device/network data for ad delivery, measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • Device controls: Android provides settings that may allow you to limit ad personalization or reset advertising identifiers (availability depends on Android/device).

8) Children and age suitability

Daily Gamles is a general-audience app and is not directed specifically to children. We selected 13+ in Google Play to indicate the app is not intended primarily for children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information to us, contact catadook@gmail.com.

9) Data retention

  • Local app data (streaks/favorites) remains on your device unless you remove the App or clear its storage.
  • Aggregate metrics may be retained as needed to maintain global statistics.
  • Server logs may be retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting.
  • AdMob data is retained according to Google’s policies.

10) Security

We take reasonable measures appropriate for an individual developer to protect the App and our services. No system is perfectly secure.

11) Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent version.

12) Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact: catadook@gmail.com

This document is provided for informational purposes and reflects the current behavior you described (Android-only, no accounts, no push notifications, no analytics SDKs, optional AdMob ads, aggregate metrics for global statistics). If you add push notifications, analytics, crash reporting, login, or server-side syncing, you should update this policy accordingly.
